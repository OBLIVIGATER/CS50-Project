Basketball Stats

Welcome to my CS50 Final Project

About:

My name is Adam I am from London,UK. This is my final project and I decided to use a similar Layout from Week 9 flask. This web application is used to input your game stats and then compare your stats with friends, other users and NBA Players from the 2020/21 Season. You also have your own Profile page where you can post your thoughts like a social media page and then using the search bar you can find your friends profile pages to look at too. As you can see some pages have better UI and UX. I decided to leave it like this to show my development over time. The first page I created was "Add Games" and the last one was "Leaderboards", and you can clearly see the difference!

Instructions

  1. Click "Addgames" in the Navigation bar. Input your stats from the games that you play. If you make a mistake or need to change your stats visit "Settings" and click "Updategames" then find the game you want to amend.
  2. Make sure you Visit the "Information" page in"Settings" page to input your Age, Position and Team. If you want to update your profile picture you can also change is there too!
  3. After this you will then be able to compare your stats with friends and Check out their profile pages. Make sure to post updates about your season on your Profilepage.

ENJOY!
